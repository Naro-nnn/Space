Fun Facts About Space

Here are some interesting facts about space that you might not know!

  • Space is completely silent because there is no atmosphere.
  • The hottest planet in our solar system is Venus, with surface temperatures around 900°F (475°C).
  • Neutron stars can spin 600 times per second.
  • A day on Venus is longer than a year on Venus.
Planet Interesting Fact
Earth Earth is the only known planet to support life.
Jupiter Jupiter has the largest ocean of any planet, made of liquid hydrogen.
Mars Mars has the tallest volcano in the solar system, Olympus Mons.
